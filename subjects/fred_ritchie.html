Fred Ritchie

Major • 14th Canadian Armoured Regiment • 1942 – 1946

Profile: Major Ritchie was almost sent to Hong Kong in 1941, but ended up with the Calgary Regiment in Italy and commanded “B” Squadron during the crossing of the Gari River where he was awarded the Military Cross for his actions. He was the Acting Commanding Officer for the regiment during the final spring 1945 offensive into Holland.

 

Years of service: 1942 – 1946

 

Focused Subject: Gari River/Kingsmill Bridge
