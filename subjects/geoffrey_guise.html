Geoffrey Guise

Sergeant • Calgary Highlanders • 1942 – 1945

Profile: Geoffrey Guise served with the Calgary Highlanders from Normandy through to the end of the campaign in Holland. He landed with the Mortar Platoon on D-Day, was wounded in the Normandy campaign, and fought in Holland during the Battle of the Scheldt in the Fall of 1944 and in the Spring of 1945.

 

Years of service: 1941 – 1945
