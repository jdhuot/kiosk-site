Jim Clacherie

Petty Officer ERA (Engine Room Artificer) • Royal Canadian Navy • 1943 – 1945

Profile: Jim Clachrie started out as a cadet and then joined the navy in 1943. He served initially on the corvette HMCS Sudbury on the Canadian west coast, patrolling for Japanese submarines and potential invasions. Later he served on the anti-aircraft cruiser HMCS Prince Robert and was present for the repatriation of Canadian prisoners from Hong Kong. He took wonderful colour photographs of all of his experiences.

 

Years of service: 1943 – 1945
