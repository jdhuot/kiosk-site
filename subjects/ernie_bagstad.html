Ernie Bagstad

Private • Seaforth Highlanders • 1942 – 1945

Profile: Born in Stavely, AB, Ernie started out as a fisherman on a trawler boat on the west coast until he joined the Seaforth Highlanders from 1942 to 1945. He mostly fought in Italy and was taken prisoner during the Liri Valley campaign in 1944. While a prisoner, he was involved in forced labour but defied the Germans in many ways, including sabotaging railways and derailing a locomotive.

 

Years of service: 1942 – 1945
