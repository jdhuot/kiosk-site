Ian Christie

Leading Seaman Botswain • Royal Canadian Navy • 1943 – 1945

Profile: Ian Christie commanded landing craft both on D-Day and in the landings in Southern France in August 1944 as part of Combined Operations. After the Second World War, he became a Canadian war Correspondent during the Korean War, where he witnessed brutal ground combat.

 

Years of service: 1942 – 1945
