Tom Conrich

Stoker • Merchant Navy • 1942 – 1946

Profile: Tom Conrich grew up in Montreal and joined the Merchant Navy in 1942 at the age of 15. He served as a stoker, in the engine room, on two ships - the tramp steamer “Phillipe Dodge” and the oil tanker “Mount Maxwell Park”. His ships carried supplies between Halifax and Britain, and later in the Caribbean. He was part of the massive supply effort to bring the materials of war from North America to Europe during the Battle of the Atlantic.

 

Years of service: 1941 – 1942
