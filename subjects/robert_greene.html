Robert Greene

Lance Corporal • Lord Strathcona's Horse • 1942 – 1945

Profile: Bob Greene originally tried to join the air force in 1940 but was rejected due to low blood pressure and was underage at 17. After 2 years as a banker, he joined the army at North Bay in August 1942. He served on tanks in England, France, Holland, Belgium, Italy, North Africa and Germany and was on his way to Japan when the war ended.

 

Years of service: 1942 – 1945

 

Focused Subject: The Liberation of Holland
