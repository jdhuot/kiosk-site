Cameron Ware

Major General • Princess Patricia's Canadian Light Infantry • 1941 – 1945

Profile: Cameron Ware commanded Princess Patricia’s Canadian Light Infantry throughout the attempts to breach the Gustav Line in Italy in 1943-44, including battles along the Moro River and street fighting in Ortona. He led from the front, under enemy fire.

 

Years of service: 1942 – 1945

 

Focused Subject: The Gustav Line
