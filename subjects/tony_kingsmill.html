Tony Kingsmill

Captain • Calgary Tank Regiment • 1942 – 1946

Profile: Tony was the Regiment’s Maintenance Officer, and as such was the Officer Commanding, No. 61 Light Aid Detachment (the RCEME Maintenance Troop) attached to the Calgary Regiment. Joining in January of 1942, Tony saw action in Sicily, Italy, and Northwest Europe. He designed the Kingsmill Bridge, a special Bailey Bridge launched from tanks that was made to cross the Gari River at the start of the battle for the Liri Valley in May of 1944. For this action he was awarded the Military Cross.

 

Years of service: 1942 – 1946

 

Focused Subject: Gari River/Kingsmill Bridge
