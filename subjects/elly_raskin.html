Elly Raskin

Sergeant • Calgary Tank Regiment • 1941 – 1945

Profile: Born in 1919, Elly was raised on a farm in Rumsey, Alberta. His father came from the Ukraine to be one of the first homesteaders in town. He joined the Calgary Tanks in 1940, serving with ‘B’ squadron as a driver (both tanks and trucks). He took part in Dieppe, Sicily, Italy and Europe until VE day. Elly was proud of his Jewish heritage and wore his ID tags with "HEB" on them in spite of knowing what might happen if the Germans took him prisoner.

 

Years of service: 1941 – 1945

 

Focused Subject: The Italian Campaign
